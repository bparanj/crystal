Tools:

- [Burp Suite](http://portswigger.net/burp/) - An intercepting proxy which lets you modify requests on the fly, replay requests and so on.
- [Nmap](http://nmap.org/) - Useful for finding additional web servers to investigate (providing the scope of the programme is wide enough)
- [DNS-Discovery](https://code.google.com/p/dns-discovery/) - Find additional sub-domains to investigate
